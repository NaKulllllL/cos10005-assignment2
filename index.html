Why Urban Fanny?

Designed for confident dining choices

Urban Fanny is a front-end restaurant discovery and reservation platform designed for families, couples, students, professionals and tourists. Guests can browse multiple restaurants, compare cuisine types, signature dishes, prices and deposits, then choose a suitable restaurant for casual meals, celebrations or premium dining experiences.

Curated restaurants

Discover six restaurant listings with different cuisines, dishes, prices, deposits and average dining costs.

Smart recommendations

Use dietary preference, budget range and dining purpose to receive a JavaScript-based suggestion.

Simple reservations

Select a restaurant, view the deposit instantly and choose voucher or online payment before submission.
